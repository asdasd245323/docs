---
title: "Disable Hosted Connect Login"
description: "Restrict access to Connect exclusively through your application by disabling the hosted login page."
---

If you're utilizing [Connect Sessions](/guides/connect/about) and prefer to restrict access to Connect exclusively through your application, it might be advantageous to turn off the hosted Connect login. This approach is especially effective if you're leveraging Connect for a limited set of actions and aim to deter users from using it for unintended purposes.

When you turn off the hosted login option, any users trying to log in through the hosted Connect page will instead be sent to a callback URL you set up, like your application's login page.

**If you want to disable the hosted login functionality, please reach out to your customer success agents, who can make the necessary changes to your Connect settings.**
